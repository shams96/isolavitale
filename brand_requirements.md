# Aura Biosphere™ — Brand Requirements & Strategic Direction

## Role & Intent
**Brand**: Aura Biosphere™  
**Core Technology**: Aurabio™  
**Positioning**: Bio-adaptive luxury skincare. Science-luxury, affluent, Europe-led.  
**Mindset**: Timeless, restrained, intelligent, inevitable. "Quiet Confidence."

## 1. Brand Foundation (Non-Negotiable)
- **Trademark Usage**: Always display ™ on first reference (Aura Biosphere™, Aurabio™).
- **Target Audience**: Affluent, ingredient-literate, global, trend-averse.
- **Anti-Patterns** (Strictly Prohibited):
    - Pop-ups or countdown timers.
    - Urgency ("Buy Now", "Only 2 left").
    - Influencer imagery or hype language.
    - Neon colors or chaotic "Grids".

## 2. Visual & Aesthetic Direction
**Theme**: "The Inevitability of Timeless Skin"
- **Key Characteristics**:
    - Ultra-minimal editorial layouts.
    - High negative space (luxury breathing room).
    - Typography-led storytelling.
    - Slow, intentional micro-motion.
- **Imagery**:
    - Abstract macro textures (cream, stone, glass, water).
    - High pixel density, neutral lighting.
    - No faces unless abstracted.

## 3. Design System
### Color System (Pantone-Aligned)
| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| **Primary** | Obsidian Black | `#0a0a0a` | Backgrounds, Footer, Contrast Elements |
| **Primary** | Lunar Ivory | `#f4f3ef` | Text, Sections, "Paper" feel |
| **Accent** | Future Dusk | `#b8a99a` | Dividers, Sub-headers, Micro-interactions |

*Rule: No gradients. No neon.*

### Typography
- **Primary Serif**: `Bodoni Moda` (Headlines, Brand Statements). High-contrast fashion heritage.
- **Secondary Sans**: `Inter` (Body, Science, UI). Clean, neutral, clinical.

## 4. Site Structure & Content Strategy

### Homepage
- **Hero**: Single editorial statement. No carousel.
- **Aurabio™ Tech**: Visual explanation of bio-adaptive intelligence.
- **The System**: Products displayed as a ritual. Price revealed on hover/interaction only.
- **Editorial**: Heritage and formulation stories (Italian Craft).

### Product Page Requirements
- Minimal still-life image.
- One-line "Product Truth".
- Texture & Sensory profil.e
- **Refill Priority**: Refill options given equal prominence to full units.

## 5. Technical Requirements
- **Stack**: Next.js (App Router).
- **Styling**: CSS Modules (Vanilla CSS) for pixel-perfect control.
- **Performance**: Zero CLS, Headless-ready structure.
- **Accessibility**: High standard without compromising visual restraint.

## 6. UX Principles
- **Motion**: Slow, fluid, invisible.
- **Navigation**: Minimal top menu (Aurabio™, The System, Journal).
- **Interaction**: Hover states should be subtle (opacity shifts, slight scale) rather than jerky or loud.
